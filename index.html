🚨 Quick Fixes

Audacity Crashed

Stay calm. Reopen Audacity for auto-recovery. Check: ~/Library/Application Support/audacity/AutoSave

Audio Quality Issues

Reconnect mic • Adjust gain to -12dB • Check room acoustics • Add pop filter if needed

Forgot to Record

Note your content immediately • Re-record while fresh • Mark as "Take 2" • Learn and move forward
